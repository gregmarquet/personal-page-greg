My story

Grew up in Paris, France. Spent a good amount of my vacations in Roscoff, Brittany where I ate the best crêpes in the world.

Realized I could change people's mood through physical comedy. Ran away and joined the Circus. Was told that I was tall and a little old so it would be hard. Worked super hard, ate a lot of pasta. Did the double backflip in my second year.

Created an acrobatic act with 2 close friends. Toured the US in 1999 with Barnum's Kaleidoscape. Learned Italian while teaching acrobatics. Ate the best home-made pasta to date.

Went back to Europe to tour the varieties in Germany. Learned enough German to survive. Discovered the great Russian writers. Learned Balalaika with Micha the great.

Came to Seattle for a three month contract with Teatro Zinzanni. This contract turned into a ten year long adventure. Performed in front of a million spectators. Learned a lot about customer satisfaction. Fell in love at first sight with an amazing woman. Moved back and forth between Seattle and San Francisco. Got married on two different continents with the same amazing woman.

Became an Art agent to promote my friend's work. Became an American. Yeah! Read a book a week for a year. Read even more now that I stopped counting.

Realized I could change people's life through technology and entrepreneurship. Discovered the work of Steve Blank and Eric Ries. Moved to New York to join a growing and exciting startup scene. Studied design and front-end development at General Assembly, wanted more, studied full-stack developement at the Flatiron School. Now ready to change the world through technology!
